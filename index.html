PoseDetect! - Lite

PoseDetect is a sitting posture monitoring app that alerts you when you are sitting in a bad posture. By correcting your sitting posture, it will not only increase your productivity, it keeps your spine healthy.



The goal is to ensure your shoulder stays parallel and your head stays upright while you watch your screen.

Privacy

I understand the privacy between you and the internet. No photo or video is being captured or recorded throughout this process. Everything is happening on your workstation.
